What is JDM??

Some might think of the type of cars that were popular in the early Fast and Furious movies. JDM means any car manufactured specifically for sale in Japan, and not for export to overseas markets such as the United Sates or Europe. JDM primarily refers to cars, but can also include car parts/accessories specific to the Japanese market.
